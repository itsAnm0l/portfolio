About

I’m Anmol, an India-based product and UX designer focused on bringing clarity to complex digital systems.

I care deeply about how humans interact with technology. When products grow in complexity, poorly considered design decisions create confusion, friction, and long-term debt.

My approach balances aesthetics with structure. I design with an awareness of both the visual and technical sides of a product,

I value products that respect their users, their constraints, and the craft behind them — and I bring that same standard to the way I design, think, and collaborate.
